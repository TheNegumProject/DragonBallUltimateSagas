==================Super Motions==================

Dengeki Jigoku Dama:

D, B, F, b   (2000 power) <== hold "b" , then Dark Ball will biger and stronger. 4 levels of ball.

Flash Bomber:

B, F, D, y   (1000 power) <== hold "y" , then Super 17 shoot more missiles.

MiniGun:

B, F, D, a   (500 power) <== hold "a" , then Super 17 will shoot longer time.

Energy Wave:

D, B, F, x   (1000 power) <== hold "x" , then wave will stronger
